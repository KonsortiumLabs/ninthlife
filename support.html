For Maine Coon Breeders

A founding resident can become a living showcase for the breed.

Ninth Life Cafe is being built around the vision of a future Maine Coon resident. We are not accepting general cat submissions. We are looking for thoughtful conversations with responsible Maine Coon breeders who understand temperament, health, social confidence, and long-term care.

  • We do not have the founding resident yet.
  • We are not accepting general cat intake.
  • We are preparing the room first.
  • We are seeking the right breeder relationship, not just a kitten purchase.
  • The resident's comfort comes before content.
  • The resident cat experience will be managed through timed visits, not uncontrolled foot traffic.
Discuss a Founding Breeder Partnership
Breeder value
  • Founding breeder recognition
  • Recognition connected to the founding resident, if approved
  • Professional photo and video content
  • Social media storytelling over time
  • Livestream visibility through Ninth Life Live once launched
  • Optional breeder profile or education feature
  • Responsible referral interest from guests
  • Long-term relationship, not a one-time handoff
The Cats Come First

Comfort before content.

The resident is not a prop, mascot, or marketing asset. Visits will be timed, handling will be limited, rest areas will be protected, and the livestream will only launch once the cat is settled.

Responsible Referrals

Selective interest only.

As people discover Maine Coons through Ninth Life, we may help direct serious, qualified interest toward responsible breeders through a curated referral process. The goal is to help people discover the breed responsibly, not encourage impulse purchases.

Local Partners

The right local pieces matter.

Ninth Life Cafe needs the right local pieces: bakery partners, rescue/care partners, opening suppliers, room support, livestream sponsors, and people who understand how to help a small place run well.
