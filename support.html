04 Care partners

A care-first partnership
for the first cats.

Ninth Life is looking for thoughtful conversations with responsible breeders, rescue partners, and care partners who understand temperament, health, social confidence, and long-term fit. The cats' comfort comes before content. Visits will be limited. The Sanctuary will open slowly.

Ninth Life is not an open-intake rescue. Future rescue or foster activity will happen only through approved partners, with care, health records, and placement handled responsibly.
