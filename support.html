04 Founding resident

A founding resident partnership, built around care first.

Ninth Life hopes to begin with one founding Maine Coon resident. We are looking for thoughtful conversations with responsible breeders who care about temperament, health, social confidence, and long-term fit. The resident's comfort comes before content, visits will be limited, and the room will open slowly.
