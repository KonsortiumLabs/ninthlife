Ninth Life / Support

Help build The Sanctuary
before the cats arrive.

We have the space. Now it needs to become a place.

The front will be a small cafe with house teas, lemonade, seating, and local goods. The back will become The Sanctuary: a calm, protected room for cats, limited visits, and careful daily care.

The first cats should arrive when the room is ready, not before.

Any future rescue or foster activity would happen only through approved partners, with care, health records, and placement handled responsibly.
