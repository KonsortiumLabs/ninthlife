The House Cat

Who they are

Composure, with a soft center.

The House Cat does not rush. They choose the highest chair, settle in, and let the room come to them. New guests are ignored on the first visit and remembered on the second.

Under the composure is a cat who leans into the right hand and supervises every drink that leaves the counter, just to be sure it is done properly.
