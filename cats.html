Care first

The cats are not here to perform.

The resident room is being designed around comfort first. Visits will be timed. Handling will be limited. Rest space will be protected. The livestream will only launch after the resident is settled.

Maine Coon vision

For people who take the breed seriously.

Ninth Life is being built around the vision of a future Maine Coon resident. We are not accepting general cat intake. We are looking for thoughtful conversations with responsible Maine Coon breeders who care about temperament, health, confidence, lineage, and long-term visibility.

For the right breeder, a founding resident can become a public-facing showcase for the breed: media, visitor attention, livestream presence, responsible referral interest, and a story that continues over the cat's life.
