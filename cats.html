The vision

A small group with real personality.

We would rather have a few cats who feel like characters than a room full of cats who feel like a crowd. Each resident is chosen for temperament, comfort with people, and the quiet they keep with each other.

  • One unforgettable statement resident, potentially a Maine Coon
  • Two adoptable or rescue companions
  • A cat-first environment, always
  • Care and comfort supported in part by sponsors
Cats resting, calm home interior
A promise

The cats live here first. Everything about the room is built around their comfort, and guests are welcomed into their home, not the other way around.
