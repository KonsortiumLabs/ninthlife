Our story

A small, real reason
to come downtown.

The Ninth Life is being developed by Kash Rogers, as part of a broader wish to bring new energy and thoughtful small businesses to Downtown Alliance.

Downtown has good bones and good people. What it needs are more reasons to show up, linger, and come back. This is one small attempt at that.

The idea is simple. A calm room, a few cats with real personality, a short menu, and a place worth the walk. Nothing oversized, nothing that needs constant events to survive. Something small, real, and memorable.

The cats are the emotional heart of it. They give people a reason to slow down, to take a photo, to bring a friend next time. The rest stays light on purpose so the place can last.
