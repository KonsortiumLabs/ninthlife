03 Room rules

A calmer room works better.

  • Let the cats come to you.
  • Do not pick up cats. Staff may make rare exceptions.
  • Do not wake, chase, crowd, or corner the cats.
  • Children stay with adults.
  • Outside pets stay home.
  • Flash photography is discouraged.
  • Food and drinks stay in approved areas.
  • The cats decide when they want attention.
  • Interaction may vary by each cat's mood and comfort.
