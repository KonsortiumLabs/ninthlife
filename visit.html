Cafe access vs resident visits

Come for a drink. Book time with the residents.

The cafe and resident room are related, but not the same. The cafe is planned as a public-facing space for simple drinks. Timed visits are for guests who want dedicated time with the cats. This helps protect the residents, manage capacity, and keep the experience better.

Not guaranteed in the cafe

A cat may appear.

A cat may sometimes be visible or present in the cafe area, but cat interaction is not guaranteed without a timed visit.

Why timed visits?

A calmer room works better.

The room works best when the cats have space, guests have time, and the day has rhythm. Timed visits keep the resident experience simple, manageable, and cat-first.
