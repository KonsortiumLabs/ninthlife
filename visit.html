03 Room rules

The room has rules.

The Sanctuary is small, shared, and built around living animals. Staff may end a visit if the room stops feeling safe.

  • Let the cats approach.
  • Do not pick up, wake, chase, crowd, or corner the cats.
  • Children stay with adults.
  • Food and drinks stay in approved areas.
  • Flash photography is discouraged.
  • Outside pets stay home.
