How it runs

Designed for the room, not the rush.

Visits will be designed to keep the room comfortable for people and calm enough for the cats. We keep numbers low so the space stays pleasant and the residents stay relaxed.

  • Booked in advance by timed session
  • Limited capacity, kept intentionally small
  • Walk-ins may be limited at busy times
  • Private visits available on request
  • Welcoming for families, dates, photos, and small groups
What it is not

It is not a place to work all day, a loud event hall, or a club. It is a calm room, a short visit, and a few cats worth the trip.
