Who they are

A student of the room.

Every new bag, every open door, every visitor gets investigated. The Future Resident is learning who is safe and which lap might be worth a try.

Give it a few visits. Shy turns into shadow, and shadow turns into a cat who follows you to the counter and waits to see what you ordered.

The Future Resident, mid-investigation
