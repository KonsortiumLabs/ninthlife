Ninth Life / Sanctuary

The cat lounge
is called The Sanctuary.

The Sanctuary is the back room at Ninth Life: a calm cat lounge built for limited visits, quiet observation, and time with cats on their terms. It opens only when the room and cats are ready.

03 Care standard

The room follows
the cat.

The Sanctuary is built around temperament, health, comfort, and routine. Whether the first cats come through a responsible breeder, rescue partner, foster pathway, or long-term placement, the standard stays the same: calm access, clean systems, protected rest, and no forced performance.
