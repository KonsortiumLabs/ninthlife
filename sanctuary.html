Ninth Life / Sanctuary

Behind the cafe lies
a sacred space.

The Sanctuary is the cat lounge in back: limited visits, protected rest, and carefully selected cats. It opens after the cafe, when the room is ready to support them.

The line between rooms matters.

The cafe can feel alive. The back room has to feel settled. The threshold keeps those two jobs from getting confused.

Front room

Drinks and seating

02 Care standard

A room with
standards.

Temperament, health, cleanliness, rest, and routine decide what happens here. A future cat may come through a responsible breeder, rescue partner, foster pathway, or long-term placement. The standard stays the same.
