02 The sequence

Follow the room before the reveal.

Every step protects the cats. We open in order, not in a hurry.

  1. Prepare the room

    Build the protected Sanctuary, rest areas, and cleaning rhythm.

  2. Set the care plan

    Define daily care, wellness checks, grooming, and comfort standards.

  3. Select the founding resident

    Choose one cat through careful, care-first conversations.

  4. Slow introduction

    Let the resident settle into the room on their own timeline.

  5. Open founding visits

    Begin limited, timed Sanctuary visits once the cats and room are comfortable.

  6. Launch Ninth Life Live

    Open a quiet window into the room - only when the room is ready.

03 Care standards

The residents are not here to perform.

01

Separate access

Sanctuary access requires a pass, reservation, or approved visit.

02

Protected rest areas

Quiet retreats the resident can always disappear into.

03

Cat approaches first

Interaction happens on the cats' terms, never forced.

04

Clean daily rhythm

A consistent routine for feeding, cleaning, and rest.

05

No forced handling

No picking up, waking, chasing, crowding, or cornering.

06

Comfort before content

The room follows the cats. The camera waits.

04 Maine Coon care

A special focus on Maine Coon care.

Ninth Life hopes to begin with one founding Maine Coon resident. The breed's size, grooming needs, temperament, and presence deserve a care-first approach. We are preparing the room, routine, and partner conversations before announcing any cat.
