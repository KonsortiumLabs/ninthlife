A CAFE FOR PEOPLE.A SANCTUARY FOR CATS.

Ninth Life Cafe is a mission-driven cat cafe coming to Downtown Alliance, Ohio, with a chill cafe in front and a protected cat Sanctuary in back. The cafe opens first. The Sanctuary opens carefully, with limited visits built around the cats' comfort.

01 The model

A public counter.
A protected room.

Ninth Life is a small cat cafe with a chill cafe in front and a protected cat Sanctuary in back. The front room offers simple drinks and seating. The Sanctuary offers limited visits with resident or rescue-partner cats, kept separate from the cafe side for comfort, cleanliness, and care.
