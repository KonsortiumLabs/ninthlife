The idea

Small by design, and better for it.

This is not a full restaurant, a coworking space, or an event hall. It is a calm room where a few cats live well, a short menu of drinks people actually want, and a place worth the walk downtown.

The cats are the heart of it. Everything else stays simple on purpose, so the room feels good for guests and stays comfortable for the residents.

How a visit works

Timed visits, kept calm.

Visits are limited and booked by time, so the room stays comfortable for people and easy for the cats. Good for families, dates, photos, and small groups.

  • Small groups, booked in advance
  • A short, friendly session length
  • Private visits when you want the room to yourself
  • Walk-ins may be limited at busy times
