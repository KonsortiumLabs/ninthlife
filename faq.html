FAQ

The short version: space secured, room in progress, residents not announced yet.

Are you open yet?

No. Ninth Life Cafe is coming soon to Downtown Alliance, Ohio. We already have the space and are preparing the room before announcing residents or opening visits.

Can I visit the cafe without booking a cat visit?

Yes, the cafe is intended to have a public-facing light cafe component when open. Timed visits are for dedicated time with the resident cats.

Does buying a drink guarantee cat time?

No. A cat may sometimes be visible or present in the cafe area, but dedicated resident time is handled through timed visits.

Why are cat visits timed?

Timed visits help protect the residents, manage capacity, and keep the room comfortable. The cats decide when they want attention.

Where will Ninth Life Cafe be?

Downtown Alliance, Ohio. More location details will be shared as the room gets closer to opening.

Are the cats selected yet?

No. The first residents have not been announced yet. Ninth Life Cafe is being prepared around the vision of a future Maine Coon resident, and the room will come before the reveal.

Are you accepting cats?

No general intake. Ninth Life is only exploring selective conversations with responsible Maine Coon breeders and trusted cat partners.

Why Maine Coons?

Maine Coons have the presence, scale, and personality to become part of the identity of a room. Ninth Life is being designed around that kind of resident presence, with care and temperament considered first.

How long is a visit?

Standard visits are planned as 45-minute sessions.

How much will it cost?

Preview pricing is $12 per guest for a 45-minute resident visit, $8 for kids 12 and under, and $100 for a 60-minute private room reservation for up to 6 guests. Cafe drinks are separate.

Can kids come?

Yes, with an adult. The room will have clear rules to keep the cats comfortable and safe.

Can I bring my own cat?

No. Outside pets stay home.

What is Ninth Life Live?

Ninth Life Live is the planned online window into the resident room. The Window Seat Live will launch after the space opens and the resident is settled.

Can I sponsor a resident?

Yes. Monthly support begins at $25/month, with higher tiers for supporters and businesses.

Can local businesses partner?

Yes. We are looking for aligned local partners, including bakeries, rescue/care partners, suppliers, sponsors, and operators.
