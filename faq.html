Visits

A standard Sanctuary visit is planned at 45 minutes for $12 per guest, with $8 for kids 12 and under. A private visit is 60 minutes for up to 6 guests at $100. See the Visit page for details.

Yes, once booking opens. Private visits are 60 minutes for up to 6 guests, with additional guests subject to room capacity. Join the visit list to be notified.

Residents

Not at launch. Ninth Life is not an open-intake rescue. As operations stabilize, we may work with approved rescue partners for foster kittens, rescue spotlights, or adoption events. All rescue and adoption activity would be handled through approved partners.

Ninth Life has a special focus on Maine Coon care because the breed has real needs around grooming, space, temperament, enrichment, and long-term ownership. The goal is not to show off expensive cats. The goal is to build a careful resident model around presence, comfort, and responsible care.

Support

Support helps prepare the first version of Ninth Life: Sanctuary setup, resident care supplies, enrichment, litter systems, grooming tools, livestream equipment, cafe setup, and early operating needs. The goal is to help the back room feel safe, calm, and ready before the first cats are announced.

Not at this time. We are not currently presenting Ninth Life as a registered 501(c)(3), so support should not be treated as tax-deductible. If a formal nonprofit or fiscal sponsorship structure is added later, we will state that clearly.
