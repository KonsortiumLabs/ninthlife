Build it with us

Built with Alliance,
not dropped into it.

We want The Ninth Life to be built with Alliance, not dropped into Alliance. We are looking for aligned partners who can help shape a warm, sustainable, locally supported destination.

Supply the pastries that make a visit feel complete. We want names from the neighborhood.

Rescue organizations

Help us choose, place, and care for adoptable residents the right way.

Downtown

Property owners

The right room in the right block. Let us talk about the space.

Beverage

Drink partners

Quality ingredients and simple drinks we can make consistently.

Community

Schools & groups

Local businesses, schools, and community groups who want to be part of it.

People

Operators & helpers

Operators, part-time managers, and volunteers who care about doing it well.

Why partner

A place feels local when local people make it. We would rather build this slowly with the right neighbors than open fast and alone.
