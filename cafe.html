Ninth Life / Cafe

The cafe comes first.

Ninth Life begins with a chill cafe in front: simple drinks, seating, limited merch, and partner-supported goods. Behind it, The Sanctuary opens separately and carefully, only when the back room and cats are ready.

01 How it works
Step 01

Stop in for a drink.

The cafe will offer simple drinks, partner-supported goods, and seating for paying guests during public hours.

Step 02

Book time for the room.

Dedicated cat time happens through separate, paid Sanctuary visits so the back room stays calm and managed.

Step 03

Let the cats choose.

Residents are never forced into interaction. A cat may be visible, absent, curious, or asleep. The room follows their rhythm.

02 Two connected rooms

Cafe open. Sanctuary protected.

Cafe purchases and Sanctuary visits are separate. Buying a drink does not guarantee cat access. You can stop in for the cafe side, then reserve a limited Sanctuary visit when available. This keeps the front room simple and the back room care-first.
