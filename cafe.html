Ninth Life Cafe

A public light cafe with a managed resident cat experience.

Ninth Life Cafe is planned as a small public-facing cafe with simple drinks, custom cups, local pastries, and a resident cat room managed through timed visits.

The cafe is meant to give people a reason to stop in downtown. Dedicated time with the resident cats will be handled separately through timed sessions so the room stays safe, comfortable, and manageable.
